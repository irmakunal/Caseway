The Problem

Medicine's most complex decisions are still made in isolation.

When a cardiologist encounters a rare or ambiguous case, there is no formal infrastructure for second opinions. They message WhatsApp groups. They cold-call colleagues. They spend hours on PubMed. Hours in which a patient is waiting.


Caseway gives every cardiologist on earth instant access to a global network of case experience — structured, searchable, and continuously enriched by the latest clinical evidence.

How It Works

Log once. Learn continuously.

Caseway is designed for how clinicians actually work — fast, structured, and built around real case data.

01

Log a structured case

A five-minute structured form captures presentation, imaging findings, interventional approach, and outcomes. Fully anonymized. No EMR integration needed.

02

Instant case matching

Our matching engine surfaces similar cases from the global network — showing what approaches were used, complications encountered, and what outcomes resulted.

03

Live literature engine

Caseway continuously indexes JACC, Circulation, ESC, FDA and EMA approvals, and active trials. Relevant evidence surfaces automatically alongside your matched cases.

The Platform

Two engines. One decision-support layer.

Case Network

A global cardiac case registry that grows smarter with every log

Every case entered into Caseway strengthens the network for everyone. The more cases logged, the more precise the matching. Rare presentations that once had no precedent become findable. Outcomes data accumulates into genuine real-world evidence.

AI Literature Engine

Continuous evidence scanning — delivered to your case, not your inbox

No more manual PubMed searches. Caseway's literature engine monitors major cardiology journals, clinical trial registries, and regulatory databases in real time. Relevant findings surface automatically when they match your active cases.

Outcomes Intelligence

See what happened — not just what the guidelines say

Guidelines are updated every few years. Real cases happen every day. Caseway's outcome layer shows you actual results from comparable patients: complication rates, intervention success, mortality, and recovery — structured and comparable.

Privacy First

Fully anonymized. Built for clinical trust.

No patient identifiers are stored. Cases are logged with structured clinical parameters only. Caseway is designed to comply with GDPR, KVKK, and HIPAA-equivalent frameworks from day one — because physician trust is the product.
